About Me

Hi, my name is Jade Romo! I am a certified makeup artist that graduated from the southwest institute of makeup artistry. I'm also the proud owner of my business Mariposa Beauty! I am very passionate about makeup and beauty, and have been consistently doing full-face makeup for over 4 years now. Even after 4 years I still have the same love and passion I had the first time I did my own makeup. I help people see their beauty from within. My biggest goal is to show all people no matter their ethnicity, nationality or gender that they are beautiful and unique individuals. Mariposa Beauty is an inclusive brand.
