Content Layout

Welcome

A small introduction to the page with the name of the company and a quick description of the career title.

About Me

This section will have a paragraph about the owner of the company and her credentials. This will consist of her background, who she is as a person and her beliefs. I will also have a picture of the owner and a background of some sort of makeup product or products.

Portfolio

This section of the site will have pictures of the artists work and text below explaining the style of makeup that is in the photo. Below the photos will be a brief explanation on the styles and what they are typically used for.

Contact Me Form

This section will have a contact me form linked to the artist email. In this contact form there will be a name, email, optional phone number and message section. There will also be a section that instructs people to contact her for any question or inquires about her work. At the bottom of the form will be links to her twitter, facebook, instagram and snapchat.
