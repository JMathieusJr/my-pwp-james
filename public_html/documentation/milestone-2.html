Content Layout

Welcome

There will be a small introduction to the page with the name of my clients company and a quick description of my clients career title.

About Me

This section will have a paragraph about my client and her credentials. This will consist of her background, who she is as a person and her beliefs. I will also have a picture of tmy client and a background of some sort of makeup product or products.

Portfolio

This section of the site will have pictures of my clients work and text below explaining the style of makeup that is in the photo. Below the photos will be a brief explanation on the styles and what they are typically used for.

Contact Me Form

This section will have a contact me form linked to my clients email. In this contact form there will be a name, email, optional phone number and message section. There will also be a section that instructs people to contact her for any question or inquires about her work.

Social Media Links

In the footer there will be links to her twitter, facebook, instagram and snapchat.
